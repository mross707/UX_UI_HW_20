Movie Path

Movie Path is a mobile app created to display the most optimal way to search for movies. It features an easy-to-use UI flow, perfect curation of movies and our signature Movie Review feature.

Research

We want to make finding great movies as quick and easy as possible. So to do that we need to find a way to search for movies in a convenient way. We wanted to ask some simple questions to answer our research questions: What was the last movie you watched? How do you usually find movies What struggles do you have finding movies to watch?

Problem Statement

It is difficult for people to find a good movies to watch. Often times, they struggle to find movies to watch.
